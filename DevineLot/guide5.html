Do not seek multiple lots at one go, according to the ancient books: Multiple lots will not be accurate, advices given will not be precise too; if you indeed have multiple questions, it is best to set them at least 12 days apart, if the question is very urgent, at least rest for a couple of hours in between questions.

不要连续抽多个签，古书云：多占者不准确，问出来的卦也不准了；如果确实有几个问题要问，最好相隔12天以后再抽，实在着急至少也要休息几个小时。
