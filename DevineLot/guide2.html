He answers every prayer everywhere and anywhere; ferrying everyone across the ocean of suffering to the shore of happiness.
Before praying for a divine lot, you should calm your mind and be very sincere. Therefore, you should concentrate on one thing at a time, and keep your mind clear of distractions.

『千处祈求千处应，苦海常做度人舟。』
祈求灵签前先静下心，须知诚恳为上首，心诚则灵。
故应专心一致，摒除杂念，一次仅询问一件事情。
